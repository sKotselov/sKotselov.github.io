Hello there, I'm Sam. I've always been intrigued into the spectrum of video games ever since childhood. Getting to play as my favourite characters inside a well designed world built around plot and adventuring, who wouldn't want that?

As time went on, I started to analyze every game I played to the point where I questioned myself :
"What If I were to create something like this?".

That's when I started looking more in-depth into the process of game development as a whole and that's why I'm currently studying at the University of Greenwich.

During my first and current second year of programming I've experienced many things ranging from
challenging myself and pushing my limit to polishing my communicative skills and teamwork.
As a result, I've improved by quite a lot now when i look back and I'm looking forward to the next oncoming challenges!

Other from playing or making games, I enjoy physical activity, reading books and video editing.
